About | PidgonCard

Your digital PidgonCard is a quick way to make transactions with just a pin. You can even order a physical card.


To make a transaction with someone who has a card, open up your user page and click the 'Card Transaction' button, and ask the cardholder to enter their name and 6 character PIN.


Even if you don't have the physical card, you can still use all the features of PidgonCard.
